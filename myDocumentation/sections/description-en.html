Digital construction - Agents: Description back to ToC

The basic class of agents, dice:Agent is defined in the Entities ontology. It has a relationship isAgentIn / hasAgent with activities (dice:Activity).

Agents are further classified into persons (dica:Person) and organizations (dica:Organization). All these three concepts (dice:Agent, dica:Person, and dica:Organization) are aligned with corresponding concepts in FOAF and Org ontologies.

Agents ontology also specifies a set of roles and capabilities of agents, as relevant to the construction domain. A role (dica:Role) is a subclass of role in BFO and capability (dica:Capability) is a subclass of disposition in BFO. Both of them are things that an agent does not inherently have but can become to posses. Roles are externally acquired (e.g., granted by someone else) while capacilities are internally achieved (e.g., through learning).

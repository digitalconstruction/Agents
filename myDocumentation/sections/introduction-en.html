Introduction back to ToC

In the Digital Construction ontologies the concept of Agent refers to entities capable of intentional behavior and making commitments. This means that agents can be responsible for activities or sign contracts with other agents.

This means that agents are either persons or organizations, such as companies or crews. (Software agent would be a separate concept).

Agents can have different roles and capabilities related to construction domain.

Abstract

The Agents ontology is known as one module of the Digital Construction ontology. The purpose of this module is to formalize the the information of agents who participate in the construction process, in order to support data sharing of the organizational and business information. This module can be used in organization-related information management use cases. This ontology is aligned with the existinng FOAF and ORG ontology, and also takes the top-level ontology BFO into account.

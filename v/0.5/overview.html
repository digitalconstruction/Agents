The Digital Construction Agents contain the definitions of the agents and their relations to activities. The basic class is dica:Agent, under which there are subclasses dica:Person and dica:Organization. Teams (dica:Team) are special type of information organizations and its subclasses dica:DeliveryTeam and dica:AssetOperatingTeam are terms derived from ISO 19650.
